Next-generation modulation architectures for high-speed communications

Ordinal is a UK-based semiconductor IP company developing novel modulation architectures for wired, wireless, and optical communication systems.

Our technology encodes information using structural properties of signals rather than absolute amplitudes — enabling fundamental improvements in energy efficiency, latency, and robustness across multiple physical domains.
